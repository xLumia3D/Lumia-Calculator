3D Printing Cost & Quote Calculator | Lumia
Lumia is a high-precision professional tool designed for 3D printing makers and businesses to calculate exact production costs and generate branded PDF quotes in seconds.

🚀 Key Features:
Dynamic Cost Estimation: Calculates material usage, electricity, and labor costs with surgical precision.

Maintenance & Amortization: Built-in logic to factor in machine wear-and-tear and annual maintenance, ensuring your hardware pays for itself.

Multi-Currency Support: Optimized for ARS, CLP, USD, EUR, and MXN with regional formatting (automatic rounding for pesos).

Professional Quoting: Generate clean, print-ready PDF quotes featuring your own Company Logo, business details, and custom validity periods.

Failure Risk Management: Integrated "Success Margin" to cover potential print failures and material waste.

Privacy-First & Offline Ready: All data (logos, prices, and settings) is stored locally in your browser. No data ever leaves your device.

Bilingual Interface: Toggle between English and Spanish seamlessly.

🛠️ Built for Makers:
Whether you are a hobbyist or running a print farm like Santi 3D, Lumia removes the guesswork from pricing, helping you stay profitable and professional.
